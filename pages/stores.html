Stores

POWER Stores are melting pots, in the heart of communities who look for unmistakable value. The energetic, bright, clean and friendly experience is welcoming to all and driven by passion to ensure that all guests leave in a better 'place' then when they arrived, regardless of whether or not shopped.

Staff are POWER’s greatest assets, who go through rigorous trading to ensure that the customer experience is positive.

As POWER Fashion continues to grow, it provides employment opportunities to it stores’ local communities, so that it can live the Brand dream to bring dignity to people, by offering affordable fashion.

POWER Fashion

POWER Fashion is a national value retailer, offering affordable clothing and footwear to the whole household, located in the hearts of our communities. POWER Cell is a sub department, offering cell phone handsets, airtime & data, tech accessories and electricity.
